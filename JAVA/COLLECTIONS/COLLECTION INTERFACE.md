## **Collection interface functions (it extends iterable class)**

---

### **Basic Query Operations**

- `size()`
    
- `isEmpty()`
    
- `contains(Object o)`
    

---

### **Iteration Operations**

- `iterator()`
    
- `spliterator()`
    

---

### **Array Conversion**

- `toArray()`
    
- `toArray(T[] a)`
    

---

### **Modification Operations**

- `add(E e)`
    
- `remove(Object o)`
    
- `clear()`
    

---

### **Bulk Operations**

- `containsAll(Collection c)`
    
- `retainAll(Collection<?> c)`
    

---

### **Conditional Operations (Java 8+)**

- `removeIf(Predicate<? super E> filter)`
    

---

### **Stream Operations (Java 8+)**

- `stream()`
    
- `parallelStream()`
    

---

### **Object Methods**

- `equalls()`
    
- `hashCode()`
